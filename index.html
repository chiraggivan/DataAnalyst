About Me

I am a passionate Data Analyst with a deep love for data and technology. I enjoy working with complex datasets, extracting meaningful patterns, and applying data-driven methods to solve problems. My eagerness to learn continuously fuels my journey, and I am always ready to take on new challenges that help me grow. Currently, I am expanding my knowledge in machine learning, further enhancing my ability to deliver impactful solutions and drive data-driven decisions.
As a Data Analyst, I have also ventured into app development to challenge myself by creating an Apple app for a Blackjack game. Additionally, as a passionate cook, I developed an Excel file to help store and calculate recipe costs when cooked at home. The tool allows users to select different weighing parameters (such as ml, cups, liters, gms, kg, etc.) for individual ingredients, providing an easy way to estimate the price of a recipe based on the selected quantities.
